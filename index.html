Our Story

Since 1998

What began as a single wok and a family recipe book has grown into a kitchen that still cooks the way it did on day one — fresh stock every morning, hand-pulled dough, and nothing that comes out of a bag.

Our chefs trained in Sichuan, Shanghai, and Canton before bringing their regional techniques together under one roof, so the menu moves between fiery, delicate, and comforting without losing its footing.
